Frequently asked questions

How does TruthLens detect AI-generated text?

TruthLens analyzes multiple linguistic signals simultaneously ГўВҖВ” including perplexity (how predictable the word choices are), burstiness (variation in sentence length), hedging language patterns, and vocabulary diversity. AI-generated text scores low on perplexity and burstiness because language models produce statistically averaged output. Human writing is messier, more varied, and less predictable. Our detection engine was trained on millions of verified human and AI text samples across GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, and Llama models.

What content formats does TruthLens support?

TruthLens Pro supports text analysis (paste any text up to 10,000 characters), image analysis (PNG, JPG, WEBP, and GIF files up to 20MB), and video analysis available on Pro and Enterprise plans. You can analyze emails, blog posts, academic papers, social media content, product descriptions, cover letters, and any written material. Image detection looks for generation artifacts in photos, artwork, and graphics.

How accurate is TruthLens compared to other AI detectors?

TruthLens achieves 94% accuracy across our benchmark test suite, which covers content generated by GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama, and Mistral. Short texts under 200 words and heavily human-edited AI content are harder to classify reliably ГўВҖВ” this is a known limitation of all current detectors. We recommend using TruthLens as one signal among several for high-stakes decisions like academic integrity reviews or editorial fact-checking.

Is my content stored or used to train models?

No. Content you submit is processed in memory and discarded immediately after the scan. We do not store your text, images, or video on our servers, and we never use submitted content to train or fine-tune any model. Your scan history lives only in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted to us. This is a core part of our Privacy Pledge ГўВҖВ” zero data selling, ever.

What is the difference between the Free, Pro, and Enterprise plans?

The Free plan includes 5 scans per day covering text and image detection. Pro at $12 per month removes the daily limit, unlocks video analysis, adds detailed signal breakdowns, and includes priority processing. Enterprise at $49 per month adds team seats, full API access, bulk scanning, custom integrations, and a dedicated support channel. All plans share the same core detection engine and accuracy.

Does TruthLens work on content generated by ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini?

Yes. TruthLens is trained to detect output from all major large language models including OpenAI GPT-3.5 and GPT-4, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Meta Llama 2 and Llama 3, Mistral, and Cohere. We update our detection models regularly as new AI writing tools become widely used. Our model-agnostic approach means it focuses on statistical patterns common to all LLM output, not signatures specific to one model.
