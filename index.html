🔒 Our Privacy Pledge

Written in plain English, not legalese. This is what we actually do with your data.

🚫

We never sell your data. Ever.

Your name, email, and the content you scan are yours. We will never sell, rent, license, or trade your personal data to advertisers, data brokers, or any third party — period. Our business model is subscriptions, not you.

🗑️

Scanned content is not stored or trained on

The text, images, and videos you submit for analysis are sent to our AI engine and then discarded. We do not store your content, use it to train models, or share it with anyone.

📧

No spam. No marketing to third parties.

We only email you about your account — receipts, security alerts, and product updates if you opt in. We will never share your email with other companies for their marketing.

🍪

Minimal, functional cookies only

We use cookies strictly to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. No ad-tracking cookies, no cross-site fingerprinting, no behavioral profiling.

🗂️

You control your data

Request a full export of your data at any time. Delete your account and all associated data in one click. No "processing period," no friction — immediate and complete.

🛡️ TruthLens earns money through subscriptions — not by monetizing your personal data. That's the whole model.
