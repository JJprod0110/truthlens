Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use TruthLens Pro, we may collect:

  • Account information — email address and password when you register
  • Usage data — content you submit for analysis (text, URLs), scan history, and feature usage
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited
  • Analytics data — with your consent, we use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with the app

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the TruthLens Pro service
  • To process and return content analysis results
  • To send account-related emails (confirmation, password reset)
  • To detect abuse and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To understand usage patterns and improve the product (analytics, with consent)

3. Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, which is hosted on AWS infrastructure. Content you submit for analysis is processed in real time and is not stored permanently unless required for your scan history feature.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and Google Analytics to understand how users use our service. Analytics are only loaded after you provide explicit consent via the banner on our homepage. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and database
  • Netlify — hosting and serverless functions
  • Google Analytics — usage analytics (consent required)
  • Google Ads — advertising

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to: access your personal data, request correction or deletion, withdraw consent for analytics, and export your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@truthlensdetect.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time, which will remove your personal data within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

TruthLens Pro is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website.

10. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at privacy@truthlensdetect.com.
